He asked me something along the line of, "The world of Computer Science is huge; therefore, one needs to pick which field they'd like to go into."

At one point, I told him I was willing to drop school momentarily, if it meant to be part of the software industry.

He told me, "I wouldn't do that."

Finally, he told me if I had a project I'd like to show him, and I told him about the text editor project I've done on github.
